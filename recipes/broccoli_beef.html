Broccoli Beef Recipe

Ingredients

  • 7 ounces beef sirloin, sliced into strips
  • 1 teaspoon white sugar, divided
  • 1 tablespoon ginger juice

Steps

  1. Toss beef strips with 1 teaspoon chicken bouillon granules and 3/4 teaspoon sugar in a bowl. Marinate in the refrigerator, 20 minutes to 1 hour.
  2. Combine remaining 1/4 teaspoon chicken bouillon granules, 1/4 teaspoon sugar, and ginger juice in a bowl.
  3. Mix 2 tablespoons water with cornstarch in a small bowl. Add to bowl with ginger juice mixture; stir.
